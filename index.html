Cajun Cooking

from Lafayette, Louisiana

Cajun means belonging or relating to a group of people who live mainly in the state of Louisiana
in the United States, and are descended from French people.
Cajun is also used to refer to the language and culture of these people.

Living in South Louisiana, you learn at a young age about food and what it can mean
to a community. Home is where community begins, and food is the glue
keeps it together. Cooking a gumbo is a time honored tradtion, and we would like to continue to
keep it alive! Here is a simple gumbo recipe handed down from our family to yours.

Ingredents for gumbo

  • 2plbs of smoked sausage
  • 2lbs of chiken thighs
  • Homemade stock
  • The "holy trinity" of celery, onions, and green peppers
  • Flower
  • &Oil
  • salt & pepper to taste

    • Cooking steps

    1. brown meat
    2. saute the "trinity"
    3. drain of fat then add oil and flower
    4. stir untill your flower and oil, create a roue with the color of your liking.
    5. add you trinity and meats back to the pot, then covering with your homemade stock.
